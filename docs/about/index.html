About JHOVE

JHOVE is currently maintained by the Open Preservation Foundation. Development effort is guided by the JHOVE Product Board, comprising OPF members and software supporters.

The original JHOVE project was a collaboration between JSTOR and Harvard University Library with funding from the Andrew W. Mellon Foundation for the Electronic-Archiving Initiative.
